Grilled Cheese

Description

The Best Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Texas Toast
  • 2 sliced of preferred cheese
  • Spreadable butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on 1 side of each slice of bread
  2. Place pan on medium heat
  3. Place 1 slice of bread butter side down in pan
  4. Place both slices of cheese on that bread
  5. Place other slice of bread butter side up
  6. Wait until bottom is golden brown and flip
  7. Remove when other side is golden brown and eat
